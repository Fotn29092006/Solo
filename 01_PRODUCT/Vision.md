## Content
The product should make the user feel that they have entered a system that measures and improves their life through earned progression.

Core promise:

Help the user become stronger, healthier, smarter, more disciplined, and more capable through daily and weekly progression mechanics.

Main fantasy:

- My life is measurable and progressive.
- My body, mind, discipline, and knowledge all matter.
- The system knows me and gives me relevant missions.
- My progress is visible, ranked, and earned.
