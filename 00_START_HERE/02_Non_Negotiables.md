## Content
- Product is Telegram Mini App first.
- Notifications must go through Telegram.
- UX must be mobile-first and fast.
- This is not just a fitness tracker.
- The system must support body, nutrition, recovery, mind, and language progression.
- XP must reflect real behavior and progress.
- Rank must not be based on spam actions.
- Stats must be tied to logged behavior or outcomes.
- Quests must be adaptive, measurable, achievable, and relevant.
- Penalties can create pressure but must not humiliate the user.
- Daily use must stay low-friction.
- Interface must feel premium, dark, futuristic, and system-like.
- Product logic is more important than superficial visuals.
- Architecture should remain modular.
- Development agents are workflow tools, not production runtime requirements.
