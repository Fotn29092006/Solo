## Product Identity

Solo System RPG is a Telegram Mini App plus Telegram Bot notification layer. It is a self-development RPG system, not a generic tracker.

Preserve:

- Telegram-first product behavior.
- Mobile-first UI.
- RPG progression that is earned through real behavior.
- Body, nutrition, recovery, mind, and language domains.
- Adaptive quests and weekly reviews.
- Short, system-like Telegram notifications.
- Premium dark futuristic visual direction.
