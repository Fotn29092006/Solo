## Content
MVP includes:

- Telegram auth and identity binding.
- Onboarding.
- Character profile.
- Goals setup.
- Daily quests.
- Weekly quests.
- Workout logging.
- Water logging.
- Simple nutrition logging.
- Weekly weigh-in.
- XP, levels, ranks.
- Streaks.
- Telegram reminders.
- Weekly review summary.

MVP excludes:

- Full AI coaching depth.
- Wearables.
- Social features.
- Guilds or PvP.
- Advanced computer vision.
- Fully dynamic body avatar.
- Ultra-complex nutrition engine.
- Full language system.
- Complex analytics.
