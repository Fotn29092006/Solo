## Content
Rank ladder:

- Unranked.
- Bronze.
- Silver.
- Gold.
- Platinum.
- Diamond.
- Master.
- Legend.

Rank should consider:

- Total XP.
- Consistency.
- Quest completion quality.
- Weekly compliance.
- Body trend alignment with goal.
- Recovery stability.
- Discipline score.

Rank must not be based only on spam logging.
